Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission take?

Most portrait commissions take 4-6 weeks to complete, depending on size, complexity, and my current workload. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

What are your prices?

Portrait prices vary based on size, medium, number of subjects, and complexity. Please contact me for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, I require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your commission and begin work. The remaining balance is due upon completion, before delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. International shipping costs will be calculated based on destination and included in your quote.
